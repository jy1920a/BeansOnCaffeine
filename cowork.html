Control & safety
Two permission modes
⏸ Ask before acting
Claude pauses for
your approval at
each action.
The safer default. Use when working with new tools, unfamiliar file trees, or anything you want to supervise closely. Every step is visible and stoppable before it runs.
Best for: first-time tasks, sensitive folders, unfamiliar MCPs, or workflows where oversight matters more than speed.
▶ Act without asking
Claude works end-
to-end without
stopping for review.
Faster execution for trusted workflows. Use only when actively supervising and working with known files, folders, and sites you trust.
In both modes: Claude always requires explicit permission before permanently deleting any files. Deletion protection cannot be disabled.
